WK 2

In Week 2, we maintained continuity with our project's initial vision, adapting to the unavailability of a dedicated camera by using laptop or phone cameras temporarily. To streamline collaboration and ensure version control, we established a Git repository.

As the Front-End Developer, I initiated the development of a fundamental HTML website, leveraging laptop cameras for image capture. Simultaneously, extensive research delved into design aspects, emphasizing aesthetics, color schemes, and user-friendliness to ensure a visually appealing and intuitive experience.

Our upcoming focus includes enhancing the initial setup, with the Git repository facilitating seamless collaboration. The synergy between research, HTML development, and ongoing collaboration establishes a robust foundation for our Facial Recognition Attendance Taking System, demonstrating our commitment to delivering a user-friendly solution.
