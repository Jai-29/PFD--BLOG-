WK 3

In Week 3, as the Front-End Developer for our FRATS website, I translated our project vision into a visually appealing and user-friendly interface. Building on previous research, I designed a clean and simple homepage, incorporating our brand color, Purple, to reflect a royal and luxurious attendance-taking system. Key elements were strategically formatted to enhance the user experience.

For the main attendance page, I focused on crafting intuitive buttons and interactive elements, ensuring a seamless photo-taking and uploading process. Additionally, I designed a logo using Adobe Illustrator, featuring a human figure with a fingerprint design, symbolizing our focus on a biometric system for secure data collection.

The FRATS system aims for a contactless, scalable, and affordable AI-powered solution using computer vision. The logo underscores our commitment to efficiency, scalability, adaptability, and cost-effectiveness.

Moving forward, my focus will be on refining the website's front-end and ensuring a user-friendly experience. I'll provide detailed updates on the development process, addressing challenges and implementing solutions for the success of the FRATS website project.
